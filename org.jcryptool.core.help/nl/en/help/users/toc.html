User Guide

JCrypTool is based on the Eclipse Rich Client Platform (RCP). You will recognize a lot of concepts in case you ever worked with Eclipse or an Eclipse RCP.

Beginner or not, the User Guide will introduce you in the many possibilities you do have with JCrypTool. The following pages therefore contain information about the different Editors, Views and Perspectives. And of course the crypto plug-ins, starting from Algorithms, Cryptanalysis, Games and last but not least Visualizations.

Please keep in mind that the User Guide contents have to be delivered by the crypto plug-ins. Generally this means that one plug-in contains more help, the other plug-in less. Sometimes only one language is available, and not both English and German. In case you do have any question about a certain plug-in or need more assistance it is best to contact the plug-in developer directly. In case you do not know the developer or you cannot reach him feel free to post a message in the JCrypTool Users Group.

The online help for the JCrypTool core is fully available in German and English.

Your feedback (bugs, ideas, and wishes) is very welcome, no matter whether it's for a core or a crypto plug-in. Feel free to post to the JCrypTool Users Group.
